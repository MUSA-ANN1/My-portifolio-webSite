Dermanlyk Ösümlikleri is a comprehensive offline reference application featuring the medicinal plants of Turkmenistan, based on the acclaimed 17-volume book series "Medicinal Plants of Turkmenistan."

Key Features:
• Complete database of traditional Turkmen medicinal plants with detailed descriptions
• Built-in Gemini AI and Qamar AI assistant for answering questions about herbal medicine
• Multi-language support: Turkmen, Russian, English and Japanese
• Fully offline — works without internet connection in remote areas
• Rich botanical illustrations and preparation guides
• Search and filter by symptoms, plant family, or region

The app serves as a digital preservation of Turkmenistan's rich herbal medicine heritage, making centuries of botanical knowledge accessible to everyone.

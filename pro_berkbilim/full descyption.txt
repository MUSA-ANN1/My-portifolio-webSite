Berk Bilim is an educational Android app combining two powerful tools
in one: a mental arithmetic trainer and a career guidance system based
on the Turkish Vocational Interest Inventory (Mesleki Eğilim Envanteri).

Mental Arithmetic:
Train your brain with sequential number calculations. Choose from Easy,
Medium, Hard, or fully Custom modes — set your own number count, display
speed, number range, and mathematical operations (+ − × ÷). Numbers appear
one by one, and you calculate the total mentally. The app instantly tells
you if your answer is correct.

Career Advisor:
Answer 160 carefully selected Yes/No questions from the Mesleki Eğilim
Envanteri (MEE). The app evaluates 10 career categories simultaneously,
assigning scores based on your responses. At the end, it reveals which
career best matches your personality and interests.

Ideal for students, parents, and anyone looking to sharpen their mind
or explore their future career path.

Görüm is an augmented reality-powered marketplace built specifically for Turkmenistan. It lets buyers view furniture, electronics, and other products in their actual living spaces before purchasing — bridging the gap between online shopping and real-world experience.

Key Features:
• AR product visualization using ARCore and Filament rendering engine
• Seller verification system with identity validation
• Blind review system — unbiased ratings from verified buyers
• Deal management with negotiation and offer tracking
• Real-time notifications for price drops and new listings
• Multi-category browsing: furniture, tech, home goods, and more

The app is designed to bring modern e-commerce capabilities to the Turkmen market with a focus on trust, transparency, and immersive user experience.

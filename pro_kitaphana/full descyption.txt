Kitaphana is a modern library management application with an integrated AI chat assistant called TakykAI.

Key Features:
• TakykAI — built-in AI chat assistant for book recommendations, summaries, and literary Q&A
• Dynamic seasonal app icons that change based on the time of year
• Complete book management system with categories, favorites, and reading progress
• Search and discovery with smart recommendations
• Clean, intuitive Material Design interface
• Offline reading list and bookmarks

The app reimagines the traditional library experience with modern AI capabilities, making book discovery and management both intelligent and delightful.
